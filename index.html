How to import from a screenshot
  1. Take a screenshot of your bank statement or receipt
  2. Open your Claude chat (claude.ai or this session)
  3. Drop the image in the chat and say "Extract transactions as CSV"
  4. Download the CSV Claude gives you
  5. Come back here and use the CSV tab to import it
This uses your existing Claude subscription — no extra API key needed.
